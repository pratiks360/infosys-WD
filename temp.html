  • Can I request extra training?

    Yes you can request for extra training by showing your manager why your business needs to upskill now and demonstrate the tangible results that training will bring.

    And finally explain how you will move forward after the training course.

  • Who will I be reporting to?

    You received an email on the date of joining from your HR partner. You can find the details in that mail.

    If you need any details on this, please reach out to your HR Partner.
